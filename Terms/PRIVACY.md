# Privacy Policy for SheepyBot

**Last Updated:** June 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what data **SheepyBot** (hereafter referred to as "the Bot") accesses, how it is stored, and your rights regarding your data.

### 1. Data We Collect
To function properly, the Bot interacts with data provided via the Discord API. This may include:
* **User IDs & Usernames:** To identify who triggers commands and manage bot features.
* **Server (Guild) & Channel IDs:** To recognize where commands are sent and where to reply.
* **Message Content:** Accessed strictly when a command is explicitly triggered (e.g., slash commands or prefix commands).

### 2. How We Use the Data
We use the collected data solely to:
* Execute the Bot’s core features and commands.
* Provide troubleshooting and technical support.
* Prevent abuse, spamming, and malicious activities.

### 3. Data Storage & Retention
* **In-Memory Data:** Most data processed by the Bot is handled in temporary memory (RAM) and vanishes when the Bot restarts.
* **Persistent Storage:** If the Bot uses a database to save settings, configuration choices, or custom data, that data is securely stored and kept only as long as necessary to provide the service.
* **No Selling of Data:** We do not—and will never—sell, trade, or share your data with third-party advertisers or external companies.

### 4. User Rights & Data Deletion
You have complete control over your data. You can request total deletion of your data from our systems at any time by:
1. Kicking/removing the Bot from your Discord server (which will flag server configuration data for deletion).
2. Reaching out to the developer directly via the contact details provided below.

### 5. Contact Information
If you have any questions, concerns, or data deletion requests regarding SheepyBot, please contact the developer via:
* **Discord Server:** https://discord.gg/JZC8rgUHMf
